About PlantAI

PlantAI is an AI-powered Plant Disease Detection and Advisory System designed to assist farmers, students, and researchers in identifying plant diseases at an early stage using image-based analysis.

The system combines deep learning with practical agricultural knowledge to provide diagnosis, confidence levels, and action guidance in a simple and user-friendly interface.

How This AI System Works

  • User uploads or captures a plant leaf image
  • Image is processed and normalized
  • A trained deep learning model analyzes disease patterns
  • The system predicts disease type with confidence score
  • Relevant prevention and action guidance is displayed

System Limitations

  • Prediction accuracy depends on image clarity and lighting
  • Model is trained on limited disease categories
  • Environmental factors are not directly analyzed
  • Results are advisory, not a replacement for experts

Future Vision

  • Support for more crops and diseases
  • Real-time mobile camera detection
  • AI confidence improvement using feedback
  • Integration with IoT and climate data
